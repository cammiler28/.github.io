About Me

I’m majoring in Media Production with a minor in Management, combining a creative eye with strategic thinking. I have a genuine passion for video production and editing, and I thrive in roles that allow me to both lead and collaborate with others. My experience spans Adobe Premiere Suite, Microsoft Office Suite, web development, and effective communication, skills I continue to refine in my current digital marketing internship with Sunbelt Business Brokerage in Roanoke. I bring strong creative and organizational abilities to every project, turning ideas into impactful, well-executed results. Originally from Lynchburg, Virginia, I’ve also spent time in Indianapolis, Indiana, and Syracuse, New York, giving me a diverse perspective that shapes my approach to storytelling and design. My goal is to continue growing as a creative professional, producing high-quality work that connects with audiences and leaves a lasting impression.
